Uttarakhand

  1. Devbhumi (Land of the Gods) – Uttarakhand is known as Devbhumi because it is home to many sacred Hindu pilgrimage sites like Kedarnath, Badrinath, Gangotri, and Yamunotri (together called the Char Dham).

  2. Natural Beauty – The state is famous for its majestic Himalayan peaks, lush valleys, rivers like the Ganga and Yamuna, and popular hill stations like Nainital and Mussoorie.

  3. Cultural Heritage – Uttarakhand has rich traditions, folk dances (like Chholiya), and fairs such as Kumbh Mela in Haridwar, reflecting its deep spiritual and cultural roots.

  4. Wildlife & Adventure – It has famous national parks like Jim Corbett National Park and offers adventure activities like trekking, river rafting, and skiing in Auli.
