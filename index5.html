Arunachal Pradesh

  1. Arunachal Pradesh is the northeasternmost state of India, sharing international borders with Bhutan, China, and Myanmar.
  2. The capital is Itanagar. Other important towns include Tawang, Ziro, Pasighat, and Bomdila.
  3. The state is home to over 26 major tribes and 100+ sub-tribes, including Nyishi, Adi, Apatani, Monpa, and Mishmi, each with rich cultural traditions, festivals, and handicrafts.
  4. Known as the "Land of the Rising Sun", Arunachal Pradesh is famous for its scenic mountains, dense forests, and rivers. Major attractions include Tawang Monastery, Ziro Valley, Namdapha National Park, and Sela Pass.
